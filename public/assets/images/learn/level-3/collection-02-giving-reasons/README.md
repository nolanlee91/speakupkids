This collection moves beyond choosing. Learners support an opinion with relevant examples and respond to a different reason.

## Suggested lesson flow

1. Identify the question and multiple reasonable positions.
2. Listen to characters give an opinion plus a reason.
3. Match examples or evidence to the reason they support.
4. Distinguish a relevant reason from a weak or unrelated one.
5. Complete polite agreement, partial agreement and disagreement turns.
6. Build a justified response from structured language choices.
7. Complete a machine-graded Mini Check.

The image establishes the discussion context only. Store all dialogue, prompts and correct answers in structured lesson data, never inside the artwork.
