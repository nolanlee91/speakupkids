## Curriculum rule

Each unit must be a multi-step story lesson with:

1. A short opening that establishes people, place and goal.
2. A problem or unexpected event.
3. Two or more clues/events that learners inspect in sequence.
4. Listening and reading comprehension with machine-graded answers.
5. A final inference, prediction or outcome.

These are not simple picture-description lessons and should not automatically be reused as Games content. Do not place answer text inside the image; all language content belongs in structured lesson data.
